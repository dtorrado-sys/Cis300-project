Hair Guide

Four tips. Curated for every hair type.

Tip #1

For dry hair

Layer your Laen Conditioner on damp hair. Let it sit for three minutes before rinsing for maximum absorption.

Tip #2

Scalp care

A healthy scalp is the foundation of great hair. Massage your shampoo in circular motions for 60 seconds.

Tip #3

Color-treated

Use cool water on the final rinse. It seals the cuticle and extends your color's vibrancy by weeks.

Tip #4

Heat protection

Apply the Laen Treatment before any heat tool. The AHA complex forms a protective barrier on each strand.
