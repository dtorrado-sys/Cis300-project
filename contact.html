FAQ

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. Laen ships to the United States, Mexico, and select international markets. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination.

Can hotels order in bulk?

Absolutely. We offer wholesale and white label programs for luxury hotels and hospitality groups. Use the form to select "Hotel & Partnership Inquiry" and our B2B team will reach out.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all retail purchases. If you are not satisfied, contact us and we will make it right.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Yes. All Laen products are cruelty-free and formulated with sustainably sourced botanicals. We do not test on animals.
