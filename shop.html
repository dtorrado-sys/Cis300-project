The Revitalizing Collection

Three formulas. One ritual. Each product in the Laen line is built around an AHA complex and sustainably sourced botanicals — 250 ml / 8.5 fl oz per bottle.

01 — Shampoo

Hydrating Shampoo

$38.00 — 250 ml / 8.5 fl oz

Our shampoo is not just a cleanser — it is the first step in a restorative ritual. Formulated with an AHA complex that gently removes scalp buildup while preserving the hair's natural moisture barrier, it lathers softly and rinses completely clean without that stripped feeling that most shampoos leave behind.

The scent profile is warm and botanical — a quiet luxury that stays with you through the rinse and lingers lightly on dry hair.

Key ingredients

  • AHA Complex — exfoliates scalp, removes buildup
  • Hydrolyzed Keratin — strengthens the hair shaft
  • Panthenol (Vitamin B5) — adds moisture and shine
  • Botanical fragrance — warm, clean, natural

02 — Conditioner

Repair & Nourish Conditioner

$38.00 — 250 ml / 8.5 fl oz

Where the shampoo cleans and prepares, the conditioner repairs and seals. Rich without heaviness, it adds weight and definition where it counts while leaving fine hair feeling clean rather than coated.

Apply generously from mid-length to ends. Leave for three minutes — longer for very dry or damaged hair — then rinse with cool water to seal the cuticle and maximize shine. The result is smooth, touchable, soft hair that moves the way it should.

Key ingredients

  • AHA Complex — smooths and seals the cuticle
  • Argan Oil — deep nourishment without grease
  • Shea Butter — seals moisture into the hair shaft
  • Amino Acids — rebuilds damaged protein bonds

03 — Treatment

Deep Restore Treatment

$46.00 — 250 ml / 8.5 fl oz

The Treatment is the most intensive product in the Laen line — a weekly ritual for color-treated, heat-styled, or chemically processed hair that needs serious restoration between washes.

Apply to damp hair before heat styling as a protective pre-treatment, or use as a deep conditioning mask: apply to clean damp hair, cover with a shower cap, and leave on for five to ten minutes before rinsing. Either way, the AHA complex goes to work on the hair shaft itself, smoothing roughness and reducing visible breakage over time.

Key ingredients

  • AHA Complex — structural repair at the hair shaft
  • Biotin — supports hair strength and resilience
  • Ceramides — fills gaps in the cuticle layer
  • Moringa Oil — lightweight heat-protective barrier
